Personal Injury

Personal injury is a broad area of practice that covers a broad range of various types of accidents and injuries. Common types of personal injury cases commonly include:

  • Motor vehicle accidents;
  • Slip and falls;
  • Dog bits;
  • Pedestrian accidents; and
  • Medical malpractice.

If you have been injured as the result of the negligence of others, please contact the Law Office of Brent Ellison today for your free consultation.
