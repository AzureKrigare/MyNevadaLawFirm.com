If you are experiencing issues with business dispute or navigating through complex contractual issues, contact the Law Office of Brent Ellison to see if we may be of assistance to you. Personal Injury

Personal injury is a broad area of practice that covers a broad range of various types of accidents and injuries. Common types of personal injury cases commonly include:

  • Motor vehicle accidents;
  • Slip and falls;
  • Dog bits;
  • Pedestrian accidents; and
  • Medical malpractice.

If you have been injured as the result of the negligence of others, please contact the Law Office of Brent Ellison today for your free consultation.

About Me

Attorney Brent Ellison

At the Law Office of Brent Ellison, it is our mission to provide to zealous, professional and personalized legal representation to those in need of legal help or assistance. We love helping people and are passionate about serving others. We take great pride in blending our experience and knowledge with an unprecedented level of client care. We are acutely aware of the stress that individuals and businesses experience in navigating through the complexities of the legal system and we will fiercely advocate on your behalf in fighting to ensure that you obtain justice. Brent T. Ellison is the founder of the Law Office of Brent Ellison. Mr. Ellison represents clients in the areas of business litigation, personal injury and sports & entertainment law. Brent grew up in the state of Kansas and attended both college and law school in his native state. Brent has zealously represented clients at every level of the Nevada legal system and has negotiated, arbitrated, and tried cases in which he has obtained millions of dollars on behalf of injured clients.
